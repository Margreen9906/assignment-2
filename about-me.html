About Me

Who Am I

I’m a creative individual with a passion for both art and technology. I studied Political Science (International Relations) at Kabul University, but over time, I discovered a deep interest in visual design and web development. From sketching with pencil to experimenting with digital tools like Photoshop and Illustrator, I enjoy expressing ideas through visuals and bringing concepts to life.


My Future Goals

I’m currently focused on learning front-end and back-end development, with the goal of becoming a skilled full-stack developer. I’m especially interested in user experience design and creating responsive, user-friendly websites. In the future, I hope to combine my artistic skills and coding knowledge to build creative projects, earn income online, and inspire others through my work
